About Us

The Appetite momos is a quick service restaurant offering vide variety of delicious and authentic momo and beverages to diners at affordable price. Momo is a type of steamed or fried dumpling with some form of vegetarian or non vegetarian fillings and highly popular in Indian subcontinent.


Our first outlet was opened in thane in 2011. In 2014 we opened our 2 nd outlet at Kanjur Marg. In 2015 our thane outlet was shifted to more spacious place to accommodate increasing customer inflow. Currently we have 4 outlets at Thane, Kanjur Marg, Airoli and Brahmand.


Our Momo is made with finest quality of ingredients in highly hygienic kitchen. We follow high quality standards in production. We offer vide variety of steamed or pan fried momo with different fillings in vegetable or meat.
